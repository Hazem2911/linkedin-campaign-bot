Claude wrote 400 lines for me. I deleted every single one.

Not because the code was bad. It was clean.

Because I asked for the wrong thing.

I said: "add caching."
I never said what to cache. Or for how long. Or where.

So I got a beautiful answer
to a question nobody asked.

Now I write 5 short lines before every prompt:
what I want, where it goes, and what "done" means.

400 lines deleted. 38 lines shipped. Lesson learned forever.

What's your worst "I asked wrong" story? 👇
